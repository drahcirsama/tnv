School Directions

From Sannomiya station,

Walk south on the west side of Flower Road about 6 minutes past Center Street. Turn right and you will see a Shell gas station. That is Edomachi street. Go south on that street one block and the Takasago Building is on the corner. We are on the 6th floor.

各線三ノ宮駅より、「花時計」を目指してフラワーロードを南へ。江戸町筋に入り、１ブロック進む。 ベルエベル美容専門学校の東向い、北側に大きく「100BAN」と書かれた高砂ビルの６階にあります
