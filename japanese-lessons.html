Japanese Lessons in Kobe

Japanese Lessons are hard to take when you are working full time!

If a foreigner wants to study the language in Japan, they have to really commit themselves and study very intensively as well as pay a lot of money for an extended period of time. But there are many people who work here full time and still want to take lessons part time!

There are volunteer teachers but they can sometimes be unreliable in terms of scheduling and lesson quality can vary too.

So here we are, conveniently located near Sannomiya Station, in the Kyukyoryuchi Area (between City Hall and Daimaru) we offer 90 minute lessons with a professional teacher on a 2 or 4 times a month basis! Lessons are available on monthly package so you can take time off whenever you want!!
